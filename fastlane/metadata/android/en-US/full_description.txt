Car Coop is the mobile companion app for the self-hosted Car Coop platform.

Use it to manage everyday tasks inside a private car-sharing group:

- book vehicles with calendar and list views
- record trips, mileage, and shared costs
- track expenses and payments between members
- post updates on the group message board
- save the current parking position and navigate back to the car
- choose the server URL of your own Car Coop instance

The app is designed for groups that want a practical mobile client without depending on a centralized service.
